Z-STAR

Maximium spin

Z-STAR provides maximum greenside spin for unmatched control and stopping power. Its premium 3-piece construction gives skilled players complete tour performance, tee to green.

TECHNOLOGY

Fastlayer core

Starts soft in the center and gradually becomes firm around its edge, giving high-speed players exceptional feel and plenty of ball speed for maximum distance.

Spin Skin with SeRM

A new, thicker thermoplastic urethane cover features Spin Skin with SeRM, a durable coating with flexible molecular bonds. It digs deep into wedge and iron grooves, maximizing spin for more control and more stopping power.

338 Speed Dimple Pattern

Less drag and more lift boosts overall distance and flies straight, even in the toughest wind conditions.
